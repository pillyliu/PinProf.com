What the app does

PinProf is the go-to pinball app for becoming a better player.

PinProf helps players understand how they perform, what scores to chase on specific games, and where to focus next. Instead of juggling multiple resources, you get a single app that has everything you need.

Whether you are preparing for a tournament or just learning a game, PinProf gives you tools you need to be the grand champion.

Help improve PinProf

Bug reports, testing notes, and data corrections are welcome.

For data corrections, please include the affected game/title, what PinProf currently shows incorrectly, the correction, a source link, and a screenshot if helpful.

PinProf is a solo-run project, so I’ll review feedback as I’m able. I really appreciate everyone who has tried out the app and taken the time to provide feedback!
